About

A strategic, data-driven approach to procurement.

I am a procurement and supply chain professional with a foundation in mechanical engineering and a master's in engineering management from The University of Queensland. My work focuses on translating demand and production plans into structured procurement decisions — through rigorous spend analysis, cost forecasting, and strategic supplier engagement.

At 5B Australia, I support the rollout of pre-fabricated solar technology by managing direct and indirect purchasing, leading vendor qualification, and improving the systems and processes behind procurement. Earlier, at Siemens Energy, I planned and scheduled high-voltage GIS projects and managed inventory worth more than $650M using SAP.

I am a fully-funded scholarship recipient for both my bachelor's (Siemens Scholarship) and master's (Government of India) degrees — and I bring that same commitment to learning and rigour to every commercial decision I make.
